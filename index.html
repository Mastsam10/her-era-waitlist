I made this for my mom.

She spent her 30s, 40s, and 50s raising four boys. Cooking every meal, keeping the house together, showing up for everyone without being asked. For about 30 years she put herself last without ever making it feel like a sacrifice.

Now it's her turn. I wanted to give her something that felt as intentional as she always was for us. Something simple she'd actually want to take, that supports her strength without adding more work to her day.

Her Era started with her. It's being built for every woman who's ready to put herself on the list.
